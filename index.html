Preparing captions....

[Monika] The Hyperaudio Lite Editor makes audio and video more accessible by creating captions and interactive transcripts.

The first step is to transcribe your content.

Choose from several speech-to-text engines. On device there's a Parakeet and Whisper model, or for faster processing and longer content, you can connect to AssemblyAI, Deepgram, or to Parakeet in the cloud.

Once transcribed, you can correct the transcript and add speaker names. Just edit the transcript like you would any other text and we'll look after the timings.

Tidy up your content, strike out filler words or any unwanted text and remove gaps and silences, all while the timings stay perfectly in sync.

When you're happy with your transcript, check your captions and tweak them right in the editor.

Finally, export in a number of formats to associate with your audiovisual media. Burn in captions ready for social media use or use the Hyperaudio Lite library or WordPress plugin to embed interactive transcripts directly into your website.
