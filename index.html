Preparing captions....

[Monika] The Hyperaudio Lite Editor makes audio and video more accessible by creating captions and interactive transcripts.

The first step is to transcribe your content.

Choose from several speech-to-text engines. There's a Whisper model built-in. Or for faster processing and longer content, you can connect to Deepgram in the cloud.

Once transcribed, you can correct the transcript and add speaker names between square brackets.

Just edit the transcript like you would any other text and we'll look after the timings.

You can also tidy up your content, strike out filler words or any unwanted text and remove gaps and silences, all while the timings stay perfectly in sync with your media.

When you're happy with your transcript, convert it to captions and tweak them right there in the editor.

Finally, export your transcript in a number of formats to associate with your audiovisual media.

Use the Hyperaudio Lite Library or WordPress plugin to embed interactive transcripts directly into your website.
